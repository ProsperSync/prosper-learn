## Full Description

```
Take control of your money — no jargon, no overwhelm, just clear financial education that fits in your day.

Prosper Learn breaks down personal finance into bite-sized lessons you can complete in minutes. Whether you're budgeting for the first time, building an emergency fund, or learning how investing works, our structured learning tracks guide you from beginner to confident — one lesson at a time.

WHY PROSPER LEARN?

• Bite-Sized Lessons — Short, focused lessons on budgeting, saving, investing, debt management, credit scores, compound interest, and more. Each one takes just a few minutes.

• Earn XP & Badges — Stay motivated with experience points for every lesson you finish. Unlock achievement badges as you hit milestones and master new topics.

• Structured Learning Tracks — Follow curated tracks that build on each other. Start with budgeting basics, then move to saving strategies, investing fundamentals, and beyond.

• Daily Streaks — Build a lasting learning habit. Track your streak at 3, 7, 14, and 30 days and watch your consistency grow.

• Quizzes & Challenges — Test what you've learned with interactive quizzes. Earn bonus XP for perfect scores and reinforce key concepts through practice.

TOPICS COVERED

Personal finance fundamentals, budgeting (including the 50/30/20 rule and zero-based budgeting), saving strategies, emergency funds, investing basics, debt management, credit building, compound interest, and money management best practices — with new content added regularly.

WHO IS IT FOR?

Prosper Learn is built for beginners, students, and young adults who want to build real financial literacy but don't know where to start. No prior knowledge required — just curiosity about how money works.

YOUR TRUST MATTERS

Prosper Learn is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases. We don't sell your data or show you sponsored content. Your learning progress is stored securely with industry-standard encryption, and you can delete your account and data at any time.

Important: Prosper Learn is a financial education app, not a financial advisor. Nothing in the app constitutes personalized financial advice. Please consult a qualified professional before making financial decisions.

Start your financial education journey today — download Prosper Learn and master your money, one lesson at a time.
```
